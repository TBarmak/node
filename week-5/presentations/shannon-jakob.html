What movie should we watch?¶

With the pandemic and all, we've both been spending more nights in than out. What better way to pass the time than watch a movie? School and life's been rough though, so we want something lighthearted, like a romance or comedy. There's a lot of different genres listed for each movie so we want to revalue each movie as either "romance" or "comedy" if they contain one of those genres, or "neither".

In [ ]:

genress = movies['genres']
comedy = genress.str.contains("Comedy")
romance = genress.str.contains("Romance")
romance[:5]

                                np.where(romance,'romance','neither'))
